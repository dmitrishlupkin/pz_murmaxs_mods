In the repository you will find folders:
PZ_original_for_reference - original game files to gain reference to code and scripts of base game. Use it to check your code and prove your solutions. Do not change it ever.
BanditsWeekOne_for_reference - BanditsWeekOne mod, that addons are based upon. Also used as reference, do not change it ever.
Other individual mod folders - different mods and addons. Work only with one you are directed to work with, do not interfere with others.
